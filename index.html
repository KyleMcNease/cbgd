Priority Workstreams

1Property / Asset Management FrameworkCritical

Gap: No policy for CDBG‑DR funded acquisitions, dispositions, or tracking—a major OIG payback risk.

Action: Adopt Texas GLO Ch. 14 "CDR Compliance" structure: lifecycle inventory, GIS‑based tracking, surplus disposition, HUD 24 CFR 570.503(b)(7) compliance.

Target Date: Month 2 (Phase 1).

2Duplication of Benefits SystemCritical

Gap: Lacks Stafford §312 compliance checks.

Action: Lift Kentucky DR Handbook Ch. 10 flow‑chart, build TN‑specific DOB affidavit + Salesforce triage engine.

Target Date: Month 3 (Phase 1).

3Financial Management ControlsHigh

Gap: Enhanced internal controls needed for DR-specific requirements including waiver tracking.

Action: Implement Federal Register waiver tracking system, vendor debarment verification (SAM.gov), cost reasonableness procedures.

Target Date: Month 4 (Phase 2).

4Monitoring & Reporting SystemsHigh

Gap: DR-specific monitoring protocols required including DRGR system integration.

Action: Deploy DRGR integration, quarterly performance reporting automation, subrecipient monitoring with risk-based approach.

Target Date: Month 6 (Phase 2).

5Procurement Policy UpdatesMedium

Gap: Enhanced procurement procedures needed for disaster recovery context.

Action: Update emergency procurement authorities, implement micro-purchase threshold guidance, create DR-specific procurement templates.

Target Date: Month 8 (Phase 2).
